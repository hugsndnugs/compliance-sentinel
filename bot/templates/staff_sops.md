## General Guidelines

### Communication
- Maintain professionalism in all interactions
- Use appropriate channels for staff discussions
- Keep sensitive information confidential

### Moderation Actions
- Document all moderation actions
- Provide clear reasoning for actions taken
- Follow the established warning system

### Conflict Resolution
- Remain neutral and objective
- Listen to all parties involved
- Consult with other staff when needed
- Follow escalation procedures for complex issues

## Reporting Requirements

Staff members must:
- Report significant incidents to administrators
- Document all moderation actions
- Maintain records of warnings and bans
- Report any conflicts of interest

- Regular training sessions may be scheduled
- Staff should stay updated on server policies

## Code of Conduct for Staff

- Treat all members with respect
- Avoid abuse of power
- Maintain confidentiality
- Act in the best interest of the community

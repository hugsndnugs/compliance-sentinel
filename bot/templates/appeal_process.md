## What to Include in Your Appeal

Your appeal should include:
- Your Discord username and user ID
- The date and time of the moderation action
- A clear explanation of why you believe the action was incorrect
- Any relevant evidence or context
- What you have learned or how you plan to improve

## Appeal Outcomes

Appeals may result in:
- **Upheld**: The original action is confirmed
- **Modified**: The action is adjusted (e.g., ban reduced to temporary)
- **Overturned**: The action is reversed

## Important Notes

- Only one appeal per moderation action will be considered
- Appeals must be submitted within the specified timeframe
- False or misleading information in appeals may result in additional consequences
- Staff decisions on appeals are final

## Grounds for Appeal

Valid grounds for appeal include:
- Evidence that the action was based on incorrect information
- Demonstration that the action was disproportionate to the violation
- New information that was not available at the time of the action
- Procedural errors in the moderation process

## What Cannot Be Appealed

The following generally cannot be appealed:
- Actions taken for clear violations of server rules
- Temporary mutes or warnings (unless there is clear evidence of error)
- Actions taken for violations of Discord's Terms of Service

## Confidentiality

Appeal submissions and reviews are kept confidential. Information is only shared with staff members involved in the review process.

## Additional Resources

If you have questions about the appeal process:
- Review the server rules and moderation guidelines
- Contact server administrators
- Ensure you understand why the action was taken before appealing
